Bible - iOS

Read the Word of God anywhere

Key Features

Easy Navigation

Tap the Book name at the top of the screen to easily navigate to a book, chapter, and verse. Or, simply swipe left or right from the main screen to go to the next/previous chapter.

Search

Search for text in the entire Bible, or narrow down by Old Testament or New Testament.

watchOS App

Includes a watchOS App.

Bookmarks

Add as many bookmarks as you wish, to easily return to later.

Reading Plans

Add a reading plan with a start date of your choosing, to easily keep track of daily Bible reading.

History

View recent history.

Copy/Highlight

Tap a verse or group of verses to copy or highlight them.

Tag

Tag your favorite verses.

King James Version

This app contains the King James version of the Bible.
